            Beds

            To many of us, it is one of the centerpieces of our home – our bed. There are only a few things that are comparable to a good night’s sleep. So, before investing into a new bed take the time and research your options before buying.

            Bed types

            Do you love the timeless style of metal bed frames or prefer upholstered beds? How about some extra storage options by buying a divan base with space for extra linen and your heavy tog duvets for winter?

            Whether you are looking for a handy TV bed or a practical storage bed, if it is supposed to be a sofa bed or an adjustable King size, at Wayfair you will find a collection that houses all different styles in different sizes at different prices to order online. From single and double beds with slatted frame to bunk beds for kids or stylish futons for your guest bedroom, our range of beds includes wooden, metal, with four poster as well as leather frames.

            Mattresses & bed sizes

            When you have picked the right type and frame, we recommend to order your bed together with a mattress to ensure the best body support possible. With some beds, like many divan (or box-spring) beds, mattresses are already included. If you have back issues, choose an orthopedic mattress or one with memory foam for a restful night. If you need some info how to choose the right mattress, check out our Mattress Buying Guide.

            When it comes to bed frame and mattress sizes, you have a wide range available. Go big with a 6’0 Super King if your bedroom is spacious or make the most of 3’0 Single if space is an issue by opting for a fold-away bed which is ideal for guests staying over. Beds come in the following sizes:

            • 2'6 Small single bed
            • 3'0 Single bed & EU single bed
            • 4'0 Small double bed
            • 4'6 Double bed & EU double bed
            • 5'0 King size bed
            • 5'2 EU king size bed
            • 6'0 Super king size bed

            For metric measurements and info on mattress depth, please check out our Bed and Mattress Size Guide.

            Design & bedding accessories

            Apart from the right size of the new bed you buy, you would like a design that matches your personal taste as well. If you are looking for a more traditional bed, choose a solid oak frame that will never go out of style yet offers comfortable support. If you are rather looking for a more modern and contemporary style, try a classic colour such as navy blue or gray or add a headboard to your chosen bed frame.

            And don’t forget about bedding accessories that are adding that extra bit of comfort and style to your bedroom. Think matching sheets, linens and pillow covers or cosy throws in similar colours. Our Filled Bedding Guide and the Pillow Buying Guide can help you choosing your new bedding.

            Often you will also find other bedroom furniture that belongs to the same collection as your new bed, such as drawers and closets. Start with one piece and then gradually add the next to create the bedroom of your dreams.

We carry the best Beds products, so you can find one that is just right for you. With our large selection of home goods, you're likely to find something that you'll love.
